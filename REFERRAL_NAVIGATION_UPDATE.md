## 🔍 Finding the Links

### As User:
1. **Login** to your account
2. Look at **top menu bar** → Click "💰 Referral"
   
   OR
   
3. Click your **profile avatar** → Select "💰 Referral"

   OR
   
4. Click your **profile avatar** → Open dropdown → Click "💰 Referral" di sidebar

### As Admin:
1. **Login** as admin
2. Go to **Admin Panel** (`/admin`)
3. Look at **left sidebar**
4. Find **"Referral System"** (dengan icon 🎁)
5. Click untuk akses dashboard atau payout requests
