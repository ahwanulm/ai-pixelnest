## 🎨 Notification Type Guidelines

### When to Use Each Type:

#### **Info (ℹ️) - Blue**
- General announcements
- Educational content
- New features (non-critical)
- Tips and tutorials
- Use: 50% of notifications

#### **Success (✓) - Green**
- New AI models added
- Promo codes & bonuses
- Feature launches
- Achievements
- Positive updates
- Use: 30% of notifications

#### **Warning (⚠️) - Yellow**
- Scheduled maintenance
- Credit balance low
- Limited time offers
- Important deadlines
- System changes
- Use: 15% of notifications

#### **Error (✕) - Red**
- Service disruptions
- Critical issues
- Urgent action required
- Security alerts
- Use: 5% of notifications (minimize!)

### **High Priority** (Use Sparingly)

## ⏰ Best Timing Practices

### **Send Notifications:**
- ✅ **9 AM - 11 AM**: Peak engagement for announcements
- ✅ **2 PM - 4 PM**: Good for tips & educational content
- ✅ **6 PM - 8 PM**: Ideal for promos & special offers
- ❌ **Late night (10 PM - 6 AM)**: Avoid unless critical

### **Frequency Guidelines:**
- Maximum 1 notification per day per user
- High priority: Max 2-3 per week
- Educational content: 1-2 per week
- Promos: 1 per week maximum
- Space notifications 24h apart minimum

## 📝 Writing Best Practices

### **Title (Max 50 characters):**
- ✅ Start with emoji for visual appeal
- ✅ Be clear and specific
- ✅ Create urgency when appropriate
- ✅ Use action words

### **Message (Max 200 characters):**
- ✅ Get to the point quickly
- ✅ Include clear benefit
- ✅ Add call-to-action
- ✅ Keep it friendly and engaging

### **Action URL:**
- ✅ Always include for actionable items
- ✅ Deep link to specific feature/page
- ❌ Leave blank for FYI-only notifications

### **Examples:**

**Good:**
```
Title: 🎨 New: Flux Pro Available!
Message: Generate stunning images with advanced quality. Try it now!
Action: /dashboard
```

**Bad:**
```
Title: Update
Message: We have added a new feature to the platform that you might want to check out.
Action: /
```

## 🎉 Quick Start for Admin

### To Create a Notification:

1. Go to `/admin/notifications`
2. Click **"Create Notification"**
3. Copy a template from above
4. Customize for your needs
5. Click **"Send Notification"**
6. Done! Users will see it instantly

### Template Customization:

1. Replace `[dates]` with actual dates
2. Update model names to current
3. Adjust promo codes as needed
4. Personalize message tone
5. Set appropriate expiry
